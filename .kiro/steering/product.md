# Product Overview

Home Loan Optimizer - An advanced financial planning tool that helps users make data-driven decisions about loan prepayment vs investment strategies.

## Purpose

Help homeowners optimize their financial strategy by comparing the opportunity cost of prepaying their home loan versus investing the same amount in various investment vehicles (Mutual Funds, FDs, PPF, etc.).

## Key Features

- **Loan Management**: Track outstanding principal, EMI, interest rate, and remaining tenure
- **Investment Comparison**: Compare 4 investment options with tax-adjusted returns
- **Smart Recommendations**: AI-driven suggestions based on interest rate differential and risk profile
- **Scenario Analysis**: 6 pre-configured scenarios comparing different allocation strategies
- **Net Worth Projection**: Calculate long-term financial impact of each strategy
- **Tax Optimization**: Factor in 80C, 24(b) deductions and investment taxes

## Target Users

- Homeowners with active home loans
- Individuals with surplus income looking to optimize allocation
- People comparing prepayment vs investment strategies
- Financial planners and advisors
- Anyone seeking to become debt-free faster or build wealth efficiently
